Description

In 2017, Show Dem Camp launched the Palmwine Music Festival, a live music experience created as a platform to celebrate and experience Nigeria’s burgeoning alternative music scene. The festival incorporates live music with art, food, DJ sets, merchandise and of course Palmwine.
‍
The first edition in 2017 was a success with over 2500 fans in attendance. The 2018 edition witnessed over 5000 fans thrilled by sounds from Show Dem Camp’s popular Palmwine Music 2 album as well as a stellar line-up of guest artists like Teni the Entertainer, Falz, Ladipoe, MI and many others.
